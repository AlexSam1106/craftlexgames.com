About the Author

🎮

Alexander Alvarez

Alexander Alvarez is a 17-year-old Honduran who managed to complete a five-book fantasy saga in just two years. A digital content creator and video game developer, he combines his passion for technology with the art of storytelling.

His books are published on Amazon, he has been interviewed on television, and has presented his work at universities. His vision goes beyond the pages: he seeks to bring his saga to the big screen.

"My goal is to create worlds so vivid that readers feel they can live the adventures alongside Jack and his friends."
